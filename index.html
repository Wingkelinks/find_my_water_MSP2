Connecting You With Australia's Ocean Pools

What is Find My Water ?

" Ocean pools are betwixt and between places, where the boundaries between land, sea and sky blur. " - All Into Ocean Pools

Find My Water is a dive into the salty world of Australia's iconic ocean pools. Dotted along its vast coastline, discover rockpools built into the edge of sandstone cliffs; quaint, netted enclosures designed for shark-proof swimming; and historic baths and pavilions, documented with stories dating back to the convict era of colonial Australia.

A showcase of some of Australia's most noteworthy pools, with important visitor information and interesting links to take you further into the deep end, Find My Water is a launchpad for anyone with a passion to explore Australia's ocean pools.
